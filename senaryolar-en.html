Scenarios · Demo Flows

When does LifeGuard AI take action?

Below are conceptual scenarios showing how LifeGuard AI SmartHealth Watch behaves in possible risky situations.

Scenario 1

Fall & Unconsciousness Detection

The user loses balance and falls, becoming motionless for a period of time.

Watch: Detects sudden acceleration + impact + long inactivity.
LifeGuard AI: Triggers “fall suspicion”.
Panel: User is marked and displayed on staff dashboard with location.
Scenario 2

Extended Inactivity & Risk in Water

Long inactivity in water may indicate danger, especially for children or non-swimmers.

Watch: Measures low movement + abnormal water-floating posture.
LifeGuard AI: Issues alert if "inactivity risk" threshold is exceeded.
Panel: Device becomes red on lifeguard screen.
Scenario 3

Fever / Health Deterioration

A sudden rise in skin temperature or abnormal health metrics may indicate early fever.

Watch: Continuously monitors temperature and vitals.
LifeGuard AI: Creates a pre-fever alert if threshold is exceeded.
Panel: Alerts medical staff or reception with location info.
Scenario 4

GPS Out-of-Zone & Leaving Area

If a monitored guest leaves the defined safe area, the system detects it instantly.

Watch: Updates GPS data when leaving the defined zone.
LifeGuard AI: Calculates “out-of-zone” probability with false-alarm filtering.
Panel: Shows “Out-of-zone Warning” with necessary action notes.
